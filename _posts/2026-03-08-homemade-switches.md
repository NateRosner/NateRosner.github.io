Description of the Project:

This project used conduct thread and conductive materials to create our own switches. Unlike the Plushy, we were not permitted to use the Lilypad components (button and switch). Instead, we had to create our own using conductive material to open and close the circuit. I personally used a paper clip and conductive fabric as my homeade switches.  


One Tip/Trick:

  One tip I have is to continue to check the front and back of your conductive thread, so that you can see if you are hiding your running switch well and the correct mapping of every component.
