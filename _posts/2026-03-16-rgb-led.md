In this assignment, we took our RGB Loop from C programming and adapted it to Arduino code in order to turn on the Lilypad LED lights. I personally turned on the Red Green and Blue in the center of the Lilypad. 

Working Lilypad: 

![rgblily](https://naterosner.github.io/assets/img/rgblily.jpg)

A tip I would give is to actually make sure you make all the necessary switches from C to Arduino. I had some trouble at first, but once i changed printf to digitalWrite my program seemed to work perfectly. Oh, also use the autoformat.
